QUESTIONS

Before you ask.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. Day-to-day changes — text, schedule, daily content — go through the web settings panel. A developer is only needed for bigger custom features.

Is this a subscription?

No. It's a one-time payment — there's no recurring license fee to keep the bot running.

What if I need help after launch?

Every plan includes a support window (30 to 90 days depending on the plan) plus documentation, so you're not left on your own right after delivery.

Can this be customized for my specific program?

Yes. Premium includes custom branding and program-specific logic. Smaller custom requests on other plans are also welcome — just describe what you need.

Where does it actually run?

On your own Railway account, with your own PostgreSQL and Redis instances — not shared infrastructure you're renting access to.
